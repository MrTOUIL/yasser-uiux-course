Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Atlas Academy, dedicated to providing quality education in Computer Science and Cybersecurity.

TOUIL ABDEREZAK

Founder & Computer Science Instructor

ESI, Algiers, Algeria

TOUIL Abderezak is a second-year Computer Science student at the Higher National School of Computer Science (ESI, ex-INI) in Algiers, Algeria. As the founder of Atlas Academy, he combines his deep passion for cybersecurity and systems programming with a commitment to education.

Abderezak specializes in operating systems, backend development, and ethical hacking. His hands-on approach to teaching comes from extensive practical experience in algorithmic problem-solving and security challenges. He holds an international certificate from Columbia University in "Learning AI through Visualization", which informs his unique teaching methodology.

At Atlas Academy, Abderezak has created a learning environment that bridges theoretical computer science with real-world applications, particularly focusing on systems programming and cybersecurity fundamentals.

Our Mission

Atlas Academy was founded with the mission to make advanced computer science education accessible and practical. We focus on core systems programming, cybersecurity fundamentals, and algorithmic thinking. Our approach combines rigorous theoretical foundations with hands-on projects that mirror real-world challenges. Whether you're beginning your programming journey or looking to deepen your systems knowledge, our courses are designed to provide the skills that matter in today's technology landscape.
